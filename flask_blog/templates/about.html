About Me

Hi. Mehrzad here.

The roller-coaster of life has led me in two career paths. let's go through them separately:

English Teacher:

English language has been a life-long journey for me. I started learning English as a toddler, my mom taught me rudimentary vocabulary she knew and read stories, exposing me to the language. As a teenager I started doing translations from English to Farsi and vice versa, which ended up with writing and translating articles for @home Robotics Team at Islamic Azad University and industrial manuals and books for Nivan Hydrualics Co.

Some traits run in the family, as for ours, it's been teaching. I started teaching English in my first year in collegue, having one-on-one sessions and free discussion classes. Later, I taught at the University of Applied Science and Technology in Tehran, Iran. To validate myself and reach the apex level of teaching methods, I obtained the CELTA certificate, in Istanbul, Turkey. Nowadays, due to the pandemic, most of my classes are online, looking after 30+ students as the prepare for TOFEL, IELTS and GRE.
